# Credits & External References

A comprehensive list of the external sites, tools, data repositories, and guides
referenced while building the fleet trackers, engineering trackers, ship field
manuals, and automation skills for this project.

---

## The Game & Its Publisher

- **Frontier Developments — *Elite Dangerous* / *Odyssey*** — the source game; all
  in-game data, mechanics, and the player journal originate here.
  <https://www.frontier.co.uk>

---

## EDCD — Elite Dangerous Community Developers (Open Data)

The authoritative data backbone for the trackers and ship dossiers.

- **coriolis-data** — ship slot layouts, module variants, engineering blueprints
  (`blueprints.json`), modifications, and experimental effects (`specials.json`).
  <https://github.com/EDCD/coriolis-data>
- **FDevIDs** — community-collected Companion API symbols / internal item IDs.
  <https://github.com/EDCD/FDevIDs>

---

## Build & Outfitting Planners

- **EDSY** — primary ship-fitting planner; the only one that reliably encodes/links
  current builds. <https://edsy.org>
- **Coriolis** — secondary build planner and the data model behind coriolis-data.
  <https://coriolis.io>

---

## Reference, Database & Community-Data Sites

- **Inara** — engineers, blueprint pages, module sellers, tech brokers, market and
  material-trader locations, commander profile. <https://inara.cz>
- **EDSM** — navigation, system and body data, route reference. <https://www.edsm.net>
- **Spansh** — route and plotter tools (neutron / road-to-riches style planning).
  <https://www.spansh.co.uk>
- **Elite Dangerous Fandom Wiki** — material identities, mechanics, and lore
  cross-checks. <https://elite-dangerous.fandom.com>
- **EDGalaxy (HGE finder)** — live High Grade Emission signal lookup.
  <https://edgalaxy.net/hge>

---

## Companion, Journal & Material-Tracking Apps

- **EDDiscovery** — journal companion; source of the SLEF loadout exports that feed
  the Fleet Tracker. <https://github.com/EDDiscovery/EDDiscovery>
- **EDEngineer** — ship-side material tracking; its `blueprints.json` is the source
  for experimental-effect data. <https://github.com/msarilar/EDEngineer>
- **ED Odyssey Materials Helper** — on-foot (Odyssey) material tracking. (by jixxed)
- **ed-nav** — third-party surface-navigation tool referenced in the Crystalline
  Shards farm guide. <https://github.com/s4nby/ed-nav>
- **EDISON / EDbearing** — surface lat/long steering aids used for shard-site
  navigation. *(Listed by name — canonical URLs unverified; confirm before
  publishing.)*
