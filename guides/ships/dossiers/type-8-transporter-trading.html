Role & Overview

The Type-8 Transporter is Lakon's modern medium freighter, and it quietly rewrote the rules: it carries over 400 tonnes — more than the larger, large-pad-only Type-7 — while fitting on a medium pad that lands at the vast majority of stations. For a trader who values flexible docking, that combination is transformative.

It's also tougher and faster than the old freighters, with enough optional space to keep a real shield alongside its big hold. It can't match the large super-haulers' raw tonnage, and at ~35M Cr it's pricier than the budget mediums — but as the most cargo you can land on a medium pad, on a modern, durable hull, the Type-8 has become the default medium trader for good reason.

Where this hull shinesFlexible high-capacity trading: medium-pad commodity runs to stations large freighters can't reach, mission cargo hauling, and any trade operation where landing flexibility matters alongside serious capacity.

Four things make the Type-8 the best medium hauler:

  • Over 400 tonnes on a medium pad. More cargo than the large Type-7 — and it lands where the Type-7 can't.
  • Medium-pad flexibility. Docks at the vast majority of stations, including many outposts — far fewer route restrictions.
  • Tough and modern. More durable than the old freighters, with room for a real shield.
  • Good speed for a freighter. A 340 m/s boost helps it escape interdiction better than the lumbering big haulers.
The ceiling, stated honestlyIt carries far less than the large super-haulers — roughly half a Type-9 or Cutter — so for pure maximum tonnage it can't compete. At ~35M Cr it's also a real step up in price from budget mediums like the Type-6. The Type-8's case is the best medium-pad capacity; if you only ever use large pads, a big freighter hauls far more.

Field Notes — What Else To Know

  • Exploit the medium pad — it's the Type-8's edge: more cargo than a Type-7, landing where big freighters can't.
  • Run it shielded; its depth easily fits a bi-weave alongside 400+ tonnes.
  • It's the smart mid-tier trader for most — only choose a large freighter if you need maximum tonnage on large pads.
  • Its good speed genuinely helps it escape interdiction — A-rate thrusters.
VerdictThe Type-8 Transporter is the modern medium hauler: over 400 tonnes — more than the larger Type-7 — on a flexible medium pad, behind a real shield, on a tough fast hull. It can't match the large super-haulers' tonnage, but for the most cargo you can land almost anywhere, on a durable modern freighter, it's become the default medium trader, and rightly so.
